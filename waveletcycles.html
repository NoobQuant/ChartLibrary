Financial cycles in European countries

Click country names to hide/show cycle.
Chart exhibits financial cycles for 13 European countries as well as for the euro area aggregate. Vertical axis can be understood as an index where values above zero imply increasing economic activity, whereas values below zero imply dampened economic activity. The horizontal axis describes time on quarterly frequency. Latest observation is currently 2018Q2. The cycles are obtained using the methodology described in Voutilainen (2017): Wavelet decomposition of the financial cycle: An early warning system for financial tsunamis - Bank of Finland Discussion Paper Series 11/2017.
